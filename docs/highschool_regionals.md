# Ando eSports Varsity Invitational - Regional Tournament Documentation

## Overview

The **1st Annual Ando eSports Varsity Invitational (2026)** is a local tournament designed for Northern Nevada high schools. The tournament aims to kickstart a grassroots esports league within the Northern Nevada region, making competitive gaming accessible to students and faculty just like traditional sports programs.

## Tournament Details

### Prize Pool
- **Total Prize Pool**: $1,000
- Prize pool is distributed among top-performing teams
- Provides meaningful recognition and rewards for student athletes
- Supports the growth of competitive esports programs in Northern Nevada schools

### Goals
The tournament serves multiple purposes:

1. **Grassroots League Development**: Kickstart a sustainable esports league within Northern Nevada
2. **Accessibility**: Make competitive gaming accessible to students and faculty, similar to traditional sports programs
3. **Program Integration**: Enable high schools to roster esports as:
   - Extracurricular activities
   - Regularly scheduled programs
4. **Student Development**: Provide structured pathways for:
   - Team play
   - Communication skills
   - Personal growth aligned with core pillars:
     - **Plan** (decision-making and objectives)
     - **Execute** (mechanics and consistency)
     - **Connect** (communication and teamwork)
     - **Control** (mental game and emotional regulation)

### Tournament Format
- Structured competitive play
- Exhibition matches
- Community showcase
- Matches hosted in a location that doubles as:
  - LAN Party venue
  - Watch Party venue
- Open to registered players and guests

## For Schools, Students, and Parents

### Benefits of Esports Programs

Esports programs offer students opportunities to develop:
- **Teamwork**: Working together toward common goals
- **Strategic Thinking**: Decision-making and problem-solving skills
- **Communication Skills**: Clear, effective communication under pressure
- **Resilience**: Managing pressure and building mental toughness through practice and competition

### Integration Options

Schools can integrate esports as:
- **Extracurricular Activities**: After-school programs similar to traditional sports
- **Scheduled Programs**: Regularly scheduled classes or periods
- **Competitive Teams**: School-represented teams competing in tournaments

### Faculty Oversight

Faculty can oversee teams, ensuring:
- Safe, positive environment
- Emphasis on growth and sportsmanship
- Balance between competitive achievement and personal development
- Structured learning opportunities
